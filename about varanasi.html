Places to visit in Varanasi

Taking participation in shilp gives you opportunity to visit prominent places in Varanasi like some of these :

New Vishwanath Temple

Situated in the premises of Banaras Hindu University (BHU), New Vishwanath Temple is definitely a must visit in Varanasi. In fact, it is a temple complex which consists of 7 temples. The Shiva temple is situated on the ground floor, whereas Durga temple and Lakshmi Narayan temple are located on the first floor. The architecture of the temple is inspired by the old Vishwanath Temple and the structure is made of white marbles. The walls of the temple are adorned with the text of Gita.

Sarnath

The Deer Park is a major attraction in Sarnath; it is believed it is the place where Buddha visited in 528 BC to preach Dharmachakra . The central monument here is Dhamekh Stupa, where Buddha delivered his first sermon to five disciples. In Sarnath, one can also visit the Archaeological Museum that displays a wonderful collection of Buddhist artifacts. The major attraction here is the Ashokan Lion Capital in polished sandstone.

Lakhaniya Dari Waterfalls

This waterfall is a kind of hidden treasure in a jungle! It is located in Chandrprabha wild life sanctuary which is around 60 mts drive from Varanasi

Gangaa Aarti

Dashashwamedh Ghat is the main ghat in Varanasi on the Ganga River. It is located close to Vishwanath Temple and is probably the most spectacular ghat. Two Hindu legends are associated with it: According to one, Lord Brahma created it to welcome Lord Shiva. According to another legend, Lord Brahma sacrificed ten horses during Dasa-Ashwamedha yajna performed here.
